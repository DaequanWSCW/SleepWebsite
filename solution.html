Introduction

There are many different solutions to solve your sleep troubles, but we have found and listedthe most beneficial ways below!

1. Grayscale

Grayscale is actually proven to make your device less addictive. Grayscale makes your phone have a black and white screen. By having this on grayscale doesn’t make scrolling through social media look interesting. It also reduces the dopamine effect too. A statistic shows that Greyscale has an average device use reduction of 37 minutes per day. If greyscale is turned on at around 7pm then this is a great way to improve device time. This is usually built in on device’s software. If not then it can be installed through the app store/play store

2. App Limits

An app limit is a great way to reduce screen time, it gives awareness of usage. Research shows that time limits reduce screen time for up to 30%. This is a built-in feature in the device’s software range from PCs, Consoles. Mobile devices etc…

3. Charge Phone Outside of Bedroom

If you charge your phone outside of your room and have difficulty sleeping, you will not have a device next to you to distract you while trying to sleep. It reduces the temptation to go on your device at night.
